Our history

Our first location, the one at Clérigos street is located in the oldest wedding dress shop of Porto.
For this reason, at the moment of the opening, all the girls were dressed in wedding dresses.
The success was so high that the way of the wedding dress has been confirmed for every Saturday night (before COVID-19).
The Pinhais Store opened on December 5, 2019.
